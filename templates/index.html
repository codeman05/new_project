  • Giving full attention to complete, clear development and understanding of our clients' goals and objectives.

What We Do

Blue Heaven Technologies tests commercial and residential air filters, flat sheet air filter media, room air cleaners, vacuum cleaners, automobile cabin air filters, and industrial pulse clean systems. We can also test air filters in extreme environments such as high temperatures, high humidity and moisture.

Blue Heaven Technologies is also a supplier of specialized testing dusts for those customers that do their own testing.
